Services Provided By Us


We provide extensive menu of mental health therapy and consultation services according to individual needs. All treatments are delivered by professionals who are qualified to do so in a supportive, compassionate, and confidential environment – where you feel safe, heard, and empowered as you transit into healing. Our individual care plans are aimed at helping you to restore equilibrium and enhance your general well-being treating anxiety, depression, stress, trauma, and other problems with your feelings. We believe in the right to effective mental health care for everyone, and we are here to support you all along the road to healthier and happier life.



Personal Counseling


Private one-on-one sessions are made available to you, wherein we would attempt to explore and address your particular mental health issues within the confidentiality and safety of such an environment. The individual therapy sessions allow a person to work hard with a treating professional who focuses treatment toward that client's particular need: anxiety, depression, trauma, or pure stress from the day-to-day. The cost for a 50-minute mental health therapy session with any of our therapists is $125, which we feel is well worth investing in your future. When considering the benefits that could include long-term emotional development, stronger coping mechanisms, and a more balanced life, this money is in fact well spent.

Price: $125/session

Recovery Period: 2-8 weeks (Depends on the condition of the patient)



Group Therapy


Support-based sessions with others bearing similar burdens are safe and empathetic places wherein individuals are free to express their shared experiences, emotions, and challenges without being judged. Such group therapy settings promote connection and understanding of one another, making the participants feel less alone and a bit more empowered through mutual support and sharing of coping mechanisms.

Price: $75/session

Recovery Period: 4-11 weeks



Method for Treating Anxiety


Anxiety can be treated with specific methods using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and relaxation strategies to help one recognize and restructure common negative thought patterns that are exacerbating anxiety symptoms. CBT usually addresses the formation of healthier thinking styles, while relaxation techniques (deep breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, and mindfulness) ease the physical manifestations of stress, thereby fostering calmness and control in daily living.

Price: $150/session

Recovery Period: 3-8 weeks



Depression Management


A comprehensive approach to mental health involves the use of therapy, alteration of lifestyle, and medical help from different directions. This approach guarantees that emotional, physical and psychological needs are satisfied by using individualized treatment plans. It could contain talk therapy, suggestions for the lifestyle of exercise and sleep, nutrition advice, and if needed, medication, which provides a holistic route toward long-term mental well-being.

Price: $115/session

Recovery Period: 5-10 weeks



Adolescent & Child Counseling


Specialized care for children and teens address the emotional as well as the developmental needs of children and teens with mental health issues as their own. This care may include age appropriate therapy techniques, their family’s involvement and school involvement to best meet their overall well-being. The aim is to assist them in being aware of their feelings, develop resistance and acquire healthy coping kin in a safe and nourishing environment.

Price: $99/session

Recovery Period: Varies (avg. 12 weeks)



All Family Counseling


Practice healthy communication and solve family problems in a safe and friendly environment where all the members are heard and valued. By encouraging open communication, family therapy reinforces familial ties and can contribute towards having healthier ways of conflict resolution thus resulting in improved peace and emotional balance for the family with healthy ties.

Price: $190/session

Recovery Period: 4-6 weeks
